hello 👋

Thanks for popping in to say hi! I'm a software engineer living in the pacific northwest with 5 years of professional experience. I spent that time working my way up from manual testing to building automated test suites and eventually to front end web development. While I feel like this experience has made me an excellent programmer with an eye for quality, I would ideally like to expand my horizons into other domains of software engineering. I decided to pursue a master’s in computer science to grow my knowledge, and in the process I’ve taken undergraduate and graduate level courses on operating systems, database management, networking protocols, algorithm design/analysis, and object oriented programming. My highest priority is to continue learning and broadening my technical skill set even after I complete my degree.
